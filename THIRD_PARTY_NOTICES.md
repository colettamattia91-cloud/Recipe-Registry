## GuildCrafts

Recipe Registry was originally inspired by GuildCrafts by dkruenbo and used it as an early MIT-licensed reference point for guild profession tracking.

The current Recipe Registry implementation has since been substantially rewritten. Some visual/UI direction or addon patterns may retain historical lineage from GuildCrafts, while the current sync protocol, data model, indexing, AtlasLoot integration, pricing integration, diagnostics, and runtime behavior are Recipe Registry-specific.
